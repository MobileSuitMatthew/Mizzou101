Campus

All campus metered lots and listed parking structures are enforced 7AM - 6PM Monday through Friday unless otherwise specified.

 

City

  • Parking Pass
  • Metered

All city garages have hourly (50 cents per hour) and permit spaces which are enforced Monday through Friday 8AM-6PM. Hourly spots are payable by cash or card. Listed city lots are enforced Monday through Saturday 8AM-6PM. Street and small lot parking is available downtown but is typically low availability and is not listed.
